Good to know

Is it really free? ⌄

Yes. You can build your page, add links and products, and start selling without paying a cent. We take a small fee only when you make a sale — or nothing at all on Premium.

How do buyers get their files? ⌄

The moment a payment goes through, the buyer gets a secure, single-use download link. No manual sending, no waiting.

What can I sell? ⌄

Any digital product — presets, Lightroom filters, templates, e-books, guides, wallpapers. If it's a file, you can sell it.

What's the AI assistant? ⌄

A chat widget on your page that answers visitors' questions about your products and guides them to checkout — like having a salesperson on your bio link around the clock.

Can I use my own domain? ⌄

On Premium, yes — point your custom domain at your store so it lives on your brand.
